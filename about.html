How It Started

From the barracks
to the bakery.

Holy Sheet! Baking started with a cast iron pan, a dog-eared recipe notebook, and a lot of time between deployments. What began as a way to unwind turned into a genuine passion — and eventually, a calling.

After years of service, [Your Name] returned home and decided to do something simple: feed people really well. No shortcuts, no preservatives, no corner-cutting. Just honest ingredients, time, and care — the same values that define good service.

Every loaf, every pastry, every order is made by hand in a licensed cottage kitchen. It's a one-person operation, and that's exactly the point.

What We Stand For

The Holy Sheet! Promise

Real Ingredients

We source quality ingredients and never substitute with fillers, artificial flavors, or preservatives. What's on the label is what's in the bag.

Community First

Got Questions?

Common Questions

Most orders require at least 48–72 hours of notice. Large custom orders (sheet cakes, event packages) may need up to a week. When in doubt, reach out early — we'd rather plan ahead than turn you away!
Currently we offer local pickup only. Pickup location and times are confirmed when your order is accepted. We're looking into local delivery options — stay tuned!
We can accommodate some dietary needs on a case-by-case basis. Please note our kitchen is not allergen-free and we work with gluten, dairy, eggs, and nuts regularly. Always disclose any allergies in your order form — we'll let you know what we can do.
Payment is due at pickup. We accept cash, Venmo, and Zelle.
Please let us know as soon as possible if you need to cancel or modify. Cancellations made more than 24 hours before pickup are no problem. Late cancellations may be subject to a fee for large or custom orders where ingredients have already been purchased.
